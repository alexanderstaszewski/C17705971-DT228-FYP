Info

This project is a Touhou character detector meant to find faces in anime artwork and identify the characters out of a handful of characters from the sixth Touhou game, The Embodiment of Scarlet Devil.

These characters are: Reimu Hakurei, Marisa Kirisame, Remilia Scarlet, Flandre Scarlet, Sakuya Izayoi

The models responsible for classifying faces and identifying characters were created with ResNet50 via a Keras and TensorFlow implementation. Due to my lack of experience with neural networks, the project did not make use of RCNNs so a cascade classifier was used to locate potential faces within the images.
